Icecream

Easy and simple vanilla icecream

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
    •

    Steps

    1. Whisk together the milk and sugar until the sugar is dissolved
    2. Stir the heavy cream and vanilla into milk
    3. Churn according to the directions on your ice cream maker
