Sorbet

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of frozen mango
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
    •

    Steps

    1. Add the frozen mango to a food processor
    2. Pulse the food processor until the mango is finely shredded
    3. Add the lime juice and maple syrup to the food processor
    4. Blend this mixture until the food processor can smoothly turn with minimal resistance
    5. Once the mixture is at a creamy consistency it is ready to serve
