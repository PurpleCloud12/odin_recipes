Strawberry Smoothie

Rich and creamy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup fresh banana
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt/li>
  • 2 tablespoons pure white sugar
  • 6 cubes of ice
    •

    Steps

    1. Place the ingredients in a blender
    2. Blend until smooth and creamy
    3. Serve immediately
